About Us

With the teamwork of 4 college students struggling with life, they have created the website named Hotel Finder. The team is hard at work developing a cutting-edge hotel booking website with the goal of becoming a key participant in the mainstream booking market, alongside Agoda, Airbnb, and Trivago. Our mission is to provide a user-centric platform that seamlessly connects travellers with their ideal accommodations, providing a wide selection of options to suit a variety of preferences and budgets. We recognize the significance of providing a great user experience and are devoted to implementing innovative features and intuitive design elements to ensure a smooth booking process.

What does the Hotel Finder offer?

With the Hotel Finder platform, you can explore an extensive selection of accommodations worldwide, catering to diverse budgets and preferences. Whether you're seeking affordable options or indulgent luxury stays, our platform offers discounted rates to suit every traveler. From cozy guesthouses and boutique hotels to lavish resorts and opulent penthouses, you'll find a wide array of choices to meet your specific needs. With millions of hotels and private homes available, our platform ensures that you can discover the perfect accommodation for your next memorable stay.

What we’re doing to help the world around us?

At Hotel Finder, we are committed to sustainable travel practices that minimize our carbon footprint and support responsible tourism. Through partnerships with environmental organizations, we actively promote eco-friendly initiatives and encourage travelers to make conscious choices during their journeys. By supporting local businesses, preserving cultural heritage, and engaging in community development projects, we aim to create a positive impact that goes beyond just providing accommodation. Together, we can contribute to a more sustainable and inclusive future for travel.

Who are the creators of Hotel Finder

Driven by their passion for innovation and their belief in the power of technology, Lucas Goh, Lloyd, Nick, and Aidan transformed Hotel Finder from a college project into a full-fledged booking platform. Their dedication and vision led them to recognize the potential of Hotel Finder to revolutionize the way people search, book, and experience accommodations worldwide. With a shared conviction that their website will bring positive change to the tourism industry, they are committed to providing a seamless and user-friendly platform that empowers travelers and promotes memorable and sustainable journeys.
