FAQs

Booking Details

How can I make a booking?

Making a booking is easy! You have two options: visit our website or get in touch with our friendly customer service team. On our website, you'll find a user-friendly search function where you can enter your destination, preferred dates of stay, and any other specific preferences you may have. Once you've found the perfect accommodation, you can proceed to book online or reach out to our customer service team for personalized assistance.

What information do I need to provide when booking?

To ensure a smooth booking process, we'll ask you for some essential details, such as:

  • Full name of the primary guest
  • Contact information, including your email address and phone number
  • Desired check-in and check-out dates
  • Number of guests (including adults and children)
  • Any special requests or preferences you might have, such as room type, bed size, or specific amenities you'd like to have during your stay.

Do I need to pay a deposit when booking?

The payment policy varies depending on the accommodation provider. Some may require a deposit to secure your booking, while others allow you to pay the full amount upon arrival. During the booking process, you will be provided with detailed information about any required deposit and the payment options available.

Cancellations

How can I cancel my booking?

We understand that plans can change, and we're here to assist you with your cancellation. To cancel your booking, please contact our customer service team directly. They will guide you through the cancellation process and help ensure everything goes smoothly.

Is there a deadline for cancellations?

Cancellation deadlines can vary depending on the accommodation provider and the specific terms and conditions of your booking. It's best to refer to your booking confirmation or get in touch with our customer service team as soon as possible to determine the applicable cancellation deadline.

Will I incur any fees if I cancel my booking?

Whether or not there are cancellation fees depends on the accommodation provider and the specific terms and conditions of your booking. Some providers may have a cancellation fee, especially if you cancel close to the scheduled check-in date. To understand the potential fees involved, we recommend reviewing your booking confirmation or contacting our customer service team.
